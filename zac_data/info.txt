ZAC v0.1
by Takis Tsiberis

This is a mind training game as shown at
"The Suite Life of Zack & Cody" movie.
Click the color boxes to match the given word,
but NOT the color of the given word.

You can play a similar game online at:
http://www.purposegames.com/game/593

Music is:
Tchaikovsky's "Dance of the sugar plum fairy"
extensively remixed by Martha Decker: martha@deckernet.com
&
Shostakovich's "Dance of death" sequenced by ...

All sounds from:
http://www.hark.com

All colors by Kevin J. Walsh at:
"RGB to Color Name Mapping"
http://support.esosoft.net/counter/rgb.html
